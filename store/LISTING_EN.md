### Description (approx. 2,500 characters)

Wishflow is a companion app for anyone who holds dreams gently—for highly sensitive people, for those who resist hustle culture, for anyone learning that slow is fine.

**A Wish Is a Relationship**

Most goal apps treat wishes like projects: chase them, deadline them, fail them. Wishflow is different. Your wishes are relationships to nurture, not conquests to achieve. When you write a wish, Anthropic's AI generates a unique hand-drawn single-line illustration just for it. Your wish becomes art the moment you name it.

**Connect at Your Own Pace**

We don't believe in streaks or deadlines. We believe two minutes of connection still counts. Choose how you show up:

🌑 **Moonrise** — 2 minutes. A gentle glance, a whisper of intention.
🌒 **Half Moon** — 15 minutes. Sit with your wish. Journal, reflect, imagine.
🌕 **Full Moon** — 60 minutes. Deep presence. Full presence. Whatever you need.

Every connection is held. No judgment. No failure states. You can return to a wish after years away, and it will still be waiting for you.

**See Your Whole Life**

Wishflow shows you two maps:

• **Wish Galaxy** — A star map of all your wishes, floating in constellation
• **Life River** — Your wishes flowing through life stages (13–18, 18–25, 25–35, 35–50, 50+), showing where your dreams settle and when

These views help you understand the shape of your desires across your whole life, not just today's productivity targets.

**Designed for Privacy**

Wishflow works offline by default. Start without an account. Your wishes live safely on your device until and unless you choose to sync to the cloud. We collect no analytics. We track nothing. We never sell your data.

When you're ready to sync across devices, create an optional account. When you're ready to leave, delete everything—all your wishes, all your data, completely. We make that easy. We make it final.

**Daily Gentle Panel**

A light touch each morning. Not a reminder that you're behind. Not a productivity streak to maintain. Just a soft prompt: *Here are some wishes you've been holding. Would you like to sit with one today?*

**Fragments**

Quick thoughts, tiny seeds. Write without the weight of a full wish. Fragments live in your pocket, waiting for the moment they're ready to become something more.

**For Everyone 13 and Up**

No ads. No in-app purchases. Free forever. This is how we believe software should be: a tool that respects you, then gets out of the way.

Wishflow waits for you through every season.

**A Wish Is a Relationship, Not a Project**

Wishflow is a wish keeper for anyone who believes that slow is fine. For highly sensitive people. For those who've rejected hustle culture. For anyone learning that showing up for 2 minutes still counts.

Most wish apps are really goal-tracking apps in disguise: deadline them, check them off, feel the shame when you fall behind. Wishflow is built on a completely different belief: a wish is not a project to complete. It's a relationship to tend.

**What Wishflow Does**

Write a wish → The app's AI generates a hand-drawn single-line illustration of it → Your wish becomes art instantly.

Then, connect whenever you feel like it:

🌑 **Moonrise (2 min)** — A glance, a whisper of intention
🌒 **Half Moon (15 min)** — Journal, reflect, imagine  
🌕 **Full Moon (60 min)** — Deep presence

No streaks. No reminders that you're behind. No failure states. A wish you haven't touched in a year is still safe, still waiting, still worthy.
